About Us

About Kesar Securities

Turning Opportunities into Financial Success.

Founded with a vision to empower investors and foster financial growth, Kesar Securities Pvt. Ltd. is a SEBI-registered financial services firm headquartered in Dadar, Mumbai. Backed by a team of experienced professionals and affiliated with Ventura, we provide personalized investment strategies across a wide range of financial instruments.

From equities to mutual funds, IPOs to bonds, currency futures to corporate FDs, our diversified offerings are tailored to help clients navigate the financial markets with clarity, confidence, and long-term vision.

Our Mission

To help our clients achieve their financial goals by delivering high-quality, personalized solutions rooted in integrity, transparency, and a client-first approach.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized investment firm, known for strategic insight, exceptional service, and a deep commitment to sustainable, long-term growth. We aim to set the standard in responsible investing and innovation.

About Kedar D. Oak

With over a decade of hands-on experience in financial markets, Kedar Oak has built a reputation for delivering unbiased, research-driven stock insights to help everyday investors grow their wealth confidently and responsibly. As a SEBI-registered Research Analyst, his approach combines technical precision, fundamental discipline, and risk-aware strategies, tailored to India’s ever-evolving market landscape. Rooted in transparency, ethics, and a passion for empowering others, Kedar goes beyond stock tips — he delivers clarity, conviction, and continuous learning to investors at all levels.
